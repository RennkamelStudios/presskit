Description

Short

Rudi is a fast-paced endless runner set in a hand-drawn desert world — run, jump, dash, and survive the sandstorm.

Long

In Rudi, you take control of a camel sprinting through an endless desert full of ancient ruins, cacti, scorpions, and other hazards. Master one-tap jumping, swipe-based sliding, and the unique dash mechanic to dodge obstacles at ever-increasing speeds. But watch out — a relentless sandstorm is always on your tail, ready to swallow you whole if you stumble. With hand-drawn assets, unlockable character skins, and a desert setting that feels alive, Rudi delivers dopamine-fueled gameplay in short, addictive runs.

Features

  • One-tap controls with swipe mechanics — easy to learn, hard to master
  • Dash system combinable with jump and slide for advanced play
  • Sandstorm chaser as a dynamic, ever-present threat
  • Unlockable character skins
  • Hand-drawn desert setting with unique art style
  • Portrait mode — designed for one-handed play on the go
